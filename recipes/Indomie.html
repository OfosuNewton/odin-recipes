Indomie Instant Noodles

Description

Indomie recipes are just too many in my country Ghana,kid love them as well as adults. This food has been able to capture the hearts of most Ghanaians with a series of good products and of coure their often captivating commercials.

Like I said in the introductory page, I never thought indomies were also for adults until I made and tasted a particular recipe.I will go further to show you my favorite recipe for indomie

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Diced Carrots
  • 1/2 Cup chopped Green beans
  • 200 g Noodles
  • Noodle Follow-come spices

Steps

  1. Set a pot on heat,add two cups of water and allow to boil
  2. Dice the carrots and chop the green bean
  3. Boil eggs separately, deshell and set aside.
  4. In the pot with boiling water, combine noodles,combine noodles,carrots, and green beans
  5. Allow cooking for 3 minutes, add the follow come spices,allow another minute.
  6. Stir together and serve with the boiled eggs.
