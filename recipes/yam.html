Your Favorite Fried Yam Chips

Description

Crispy fried yam with pepper sauce (dundun) is a delicious savory dish that can be served as a starter or side. With a few simple ingredients you'll be serving this delicious dish in no time!

Ingredients

  • Peeled yam
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel wash and chop the yam.
  2. Soak the yam piece in water for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a saucepan or deep fryer and fry the yam piece in water
  4. Add 3-7 teaspoon of salted water to the saucepan and cook until golden yellow colour.
    5. The amount of water you add will depend on how much fries you are frying.Adding water to the oil wnsures that the yam fries cool on the inside and not just the outside.
